What is Fat Loss?

Fat loss refers to the process of reducing the amount of fat stored in the body. This is typically achieved through a combination of diet, exercise, and lifestyle changes. The goal is to lower body fat percentage while preserving muscle mass.

Effective fat loss involves creating a calorie deficit, where the number of calories burned exceeds the number of calories consumed. This can be accomplished through various methods such as increasing physical activity, adopting a healthy diet, and improving metabolic function.
