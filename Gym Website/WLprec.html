Benefits of Weightlifting

  • Increases muscle strength and endurance.
  • Enhances bone density, reducing the risk of osteoporosis.
  • Boosts metabolism and aids in weight management.
  • Improves overall body composition by increasing lean muscle mass.
  • Enhances mental health by reducing stress and improving mood.
