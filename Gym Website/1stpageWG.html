Information About Weight Gaining

Weight gaining involves increasing body mass through a combination of dietary changes and exercise. This usually includes consuming more calories than you burn and engaging in strength training exercises to build muscle.

Key strategies for weight gaining include eating calorie-dense foods, increasing protein intake, and performing resistance training to ensure that the additional weight gained is in the form of muscle rather than fat.
