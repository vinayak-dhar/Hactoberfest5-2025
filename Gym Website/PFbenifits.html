Benefits of Physical Fitness

  • Improves cardiovascular health and reduces the risk of heart disease.
  • Enhances muscle strength, endurance, and flexibility.
  • Supports weight management and promotes a healthy body composition.
  • Boosts mental health by reducing stress, anxiety, and depression.
  • Improves sleep quality and overall energy levels.
